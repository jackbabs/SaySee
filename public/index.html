How to use SaySee
  1. Click the speech button to start up your mic

  2. Speak into your mic to actively search for videos

  3. Click an option on the side panel to choose a different video from your search

  4. While playing a video your mic will be paused

  5. Pause the video and click the speech button to make another search!
